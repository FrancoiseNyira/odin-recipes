Chocolate Granola Bars

No Bake Chocolate Dipped Chewy Granola Bars.

No Bake Chocolate Dipped Chewy Granola Bars...exactly the snack you need in your life right now. Made using rolled oats, sliced almonds, hemp hearts, peanut butter, coconut oil, maple syrup, and a little vanilla. Plus they’re dipped in dairy-free rich, dark chocolate. These bars are vegan, gluten free, and so delicious. Just the right amount of chewy, sweet, and peanut buttery...they are the perfect grab and go snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cup old fashioned rolled oats
  • ½ cup sliced almonds
  • ½ cup hemp hearts
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ⅓ cup maple syrup
  • ½ cup creamy peanut butter
  • ¼ cup coconut oil + 1 tsp
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup dark chocolate chips + 2 tbsp (I use Enjoy Life)

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, add the oats, sliced almonds, hemp hearts, and cinnamon. Mix together until evenly distributed. Set aside.
  2. In a small saucepan, add the maple syrup, peanut butter, ¼ cup coconut oil, and vanilla. Heat over medium-low heat and whisk together. Bring to a low simmer, continue cooking for 2-3 minutes, whisking continually.
  3. Pour the peanut butter mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients and mix together until well combined and oat mixture is completely coated.
  4. Pour the peanut butter mixture into the bowl with the dry ingredients and mix together until well combined and oat mixture is completely coated.
  5. Pour the mixture into the prepped baking dish and evenly distribute it. Press down firmly with a spatula. Place in the freezer for at least an hour to chill.
  6. Melt the 1 cup chocolate chips in the microwave (30 second increment first and then 15 second increments after until melted, to ensure it doesn’t burn). For the drizzle, add the 2 tbsp chocolate chips and the 1 tsp coconut oil in a small bowl and microwave until melted.
  7. Take the chilled granola mixture out of the freezer, remove from the dish. Transfer the parchment paper to a large cookie sheet. Cut into 16 even bars. Dip them in the chocolate or use a spoon to coat one side of the bar with the melted chocolate. Set the bars chocolate side down on the cookie sheet. Drizzle with chocolate and coconut oil mixture. Place them back in the freezer for an additional 5 minutes to let the chocolate firm up. Store in the freezer or serve immediately
